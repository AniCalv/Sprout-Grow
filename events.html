Join Us!

Whether you're a curious kid, a passionate adult, or somewhere in between we have events for everyone! Join us for a variety of events hosted by our very own gardeners and experts, from fun hands-on activities for the little ones to informative classes, inspiring lectures, and laid-back community get-togethers. We'd love to see you there

Kid Events

Our kids events are designed to get little ones excited about nature and gardening in a fun, hands-on way! From planting their very first seed to learning about bugs and butterflies, there's always something new to discover. No experience needed, just curiosity and a love for getting your hands dirty! Perfect for kids ages 4-12.

Classes

Ready to level up your gardening skills? Our classes are led by our experienced in-house gardeners and cover everything from the basics of organic gardening to more advanced techniques like composting, raised bed gardening, and plant care. Classes are kept small so you always get plenty of one-on-one attention. All skill levels are welcome!

Lectures

Our lectures bring together gardening experts, environmental advocates, and local community leaders to share their knowledge and passion for sustainable living. Whether you're interested in learning about organic pest control, soil health, or eco-friendly gardening practices, our lectures are a great way to deepen your understanding and connect with like-minded people

Community Get-Togethers

Sometimes it's just nice to get together! Our community get-togethers are casual, laid-back events where gardeners of all levels can meet, share tips, swap plants, and enjoy some good company. Keep an eye on our events calendar so you never miss out!
